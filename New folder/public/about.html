About Us

Engineering Clarity and Trust for the AI Era

Founded by Deep Sodha, AxionX Digital Pvt. Ltd. helps AI labs and platform teams build trustworthy models and reliable systems.

Our mission is simple: make AI evaluations reproducible, repositories stable, and developer tooling a force multiplier instead of a bottleneck.

With a 120+ person team of senior engineers across Rust, Go, Python, Java, and JavaScript, we bring open-source rigor to enterprise-scale AI challenges. Every engagement is designed for clarity, reproducibility, and reliability.
